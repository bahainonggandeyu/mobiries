功能要求

Functional Requirements

  • 1.产品要求轻便。                                                                                                                                                              原因：老人年衰体弱，过重的产品不仅无法辅助老人的生活，反而会成为他们的负担              1. The product should be light.                                                                                                                                     Reason: heavy products will become a burden on them. 
  • 2.产品要求能够辅助老人上下楼梯。                                                                                                                                原因：老人腿脚不便，上下楼吃力。                                                                                                                  2. The product is required to assist the elderly to go up and down stairs.                                   Reason: the old people's legs and feet are inconvenient, and it's hard to get up and down the stairs.  
  • 3.产品要有座椅功能。                                                                                                                                                            原因：老人经常在路上感到劳累，有座椅功能的产品能让老人随时随地坐下休息。                   3. The product should have seat function.                                                                                                           Reason: the elderly often feel tired on the road, products with seat function can let the elderly sit down and rest anytime and anywhere. 
  • 4.产品要求有载物功能。                                                                                                                                                        原因：独居老人时常会下楼买菜购物等，提重物行走。                                                                             4. The product is required to have load function.                                                                                           Reason: the old people living alone often go downstairs to buy vegetables, shop, and walk with heavy objects. 
  • 5.产品应有防倾设计                                                                                                                                                                  原因：老年人平衡性差，重心不易控制。                                                                                                           5. The product should be designed to prevent tilting                                                                                   Reason: the balance of the elderly is poor and the center of gravity is not easy to control. 
  • 6.产品要有辅助蹲起的功能                                                                                                                                                  原因：据观察，老人在坐下站起的过程较为艰难，产品应作为一个支撑，辅助老人站立。     6. The product should have the function of assisting squatting                                                         Reason: according to observation, it is difficult for the elderly to sit down and stand up. The product should be used as a support to assist the elderly to stand up. 

调查2：产品参数

Survey 2: product parameters

我们调查了两个城市共计150栋楼、16种规格的楼梯，并查询得知规定楼梯宽度不得少于250mm。最终得出产品的最合适的前后跨度为42cm。经测试，此宽度可适应绝大部分楼梯 。右图中，纵坐标代表台阶的宽度，横坐标代表各种宽度的楼梯在样本中的数量

We investigated 150 buildings and 16 kinds of stairs in two cities, and found that the width of the stairs should not be less than 250mm. Finally, the most suitable distance between the front and rear feet of the product is 42cm. After testing, this width can adapt to most stairs
In the right chart, the ordinate represents the width of the steps, and the abscissa represents the number of stairs of various widths in the sample 

        根据《中国居民营养与慢性疾病状况报告（2015）》，我国目前中老年人最低的年龄组为55-59岁，平均身高160.6厘米。又知医学上对人体最佳的拐杖高度为人体身高*0.51，所以我们将产品高度定为74-92之间

        According to the report on nutrition and chronic diseases of Chinese residents (2015), the lowest age group of middle-aged and elderly people in China is 55-59 years old, with an average height of 160.6 cm. We also know that the best height of crutches for human body is human height * 0.51, so we set the height of the product as 74-92cm 

试验方式：用PVC管制作一个简单模型。通过往里面倒不同重量的沙子的方法控制模型重量，让消费者说出最合适的重量
Test method: a simple model was made with PVC pipe. The weight of the model is controlled by pouring sand of different weights into it, so that the consumers can tell the most suitable weight 

我们通过增加不同的配重的方式测试30个老人，寻找他们认为舒适的重量的范围。得出右表，并取得最大公约数。由调查得知我们的产品重量应当控制在2.2至3.9之间 
We tested 30 elderly people by adding different weights, and measured the range of weights they felt comfortable with.. Get the right chart and the greatest common divisor. According to the investigation, the weight of our products should be controlled between 2.2kg and 3.9kg 

        每根线代表一个受访者，横坐标代表受访者可接受的重量区间 
        Each line represents a respondent, and the abscissa represents the acceptable weight range for the interviewee 

通过改变模型中间的横杠的长短，测试用户认为合适的宽度范围
By changing the length of the horizontal bar in the middle of the model, test the appropriate width range of the user

We use the same method as the weight test to test the appropriate width of the product and get the chart above
