项目简介
Brief Introduction

本款产品旨在通过可拆卸的、模块化的产品，辅助老人日常活动，给老人以“我有足够的精力”的幻觉，从而赋予他们信心，鼓励他们走出家门进行正常的日常活动 。

This product is designed to assist the elderly in their daily activities through detachable and modular products, giving them the illusion of "I have enough energy", so as to give them confidence and encourage them to go out of their homes for normal daily activities 

项目背景
Project background

        社会状况凸显：如今，子女外出务工情况普遍，但其父母缺乏照顾，老人们即使有基本的自理能力，也会因为身体因素在处理复杂状况时面临麻烦。当今的中国农村城镇化迅速，城市交通，道路，生活方式变化巨大，对住惯了农村平房的老人来说很多出行（高楼，天桥，斜坡，坎）会很不方便 。
        The social problems are becoming increasingly serious.
Nowadays, it is common for people to work away from their hometowns ,leaving their parents at home alone with no one caring for the parents. Even if the elderly have basic self-care ability, they will have trouble with complex situations due to the physical factors. Today in China, with the rapid spread of urbanization ，Great changes have taken place in urban transportation, roads and lifestyle. For the old people who are used to living in rural bungalows, many trips (tall buildings, overpasses, slopes, ridges) will be inconvenient

材料选取
Material selection

主体框架：钛合金
Main frame: titanium alloy

其耐热性、强度、塑性、韧性、成形性、可焊性、耐蚀性和生物相容性均较好，且密度较小。预计产品所有金属管仅重2.0千克 。

Its heat resistance, strength, plasticity, toughness, formability, weldability, corrosion resistance and biocompatibility are good, and the density is small. It is estimated that all metal pipes of the product will only weigh 2.0 kg

椅面：高强度炭纤维
Chair surface: high strength carbon fiber

由碳元素组成的一种特种纤维。具有耐高温、抗摩擦、耐腐蚀等特性。碳纤维的密度小，因此比强度和比模量高。此材料制成的椅面坚固、安全、舒适。
A special fiber made up of carbon. It has the characteristics of high temperature resistance, friction resistance and corrosion resistance. The density of carbon fiber is small, so the specific strength and specific modulus are high. The chair surface made of this material is firm, safe and comfortable.

布包：防水面料（PTFE）
Cloth bag: waterproof fabric (PTFE)

高分子防水透气材料加复合面料，具有防水，透湿，透气，绝缘，防风等特点。


Polymer waterproof and breathable material plus composite fabric has the characteristics of waterproof, moisture permeability, air permeability, insulation, windproof, etc
